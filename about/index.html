About Us

A Culinary Odyssey Across Continents In a world where borders blur, four passionate individuals from Ethiopia, Ireland, Lebanon, and Hong Kong found a common language in their love for food. United by a shared dream, we embarked on a culinary odyssey to create a restaurant that mirrors our unique backgrounds. Our diverse team brings together the authenticity of traditional Ethiopian dishes, the hearty comfort of Irish favorites, the aromatic delights of Lebanese cuisine, and the bold flavors of Hong Kong street food. Culinary Concoctions isn't just a restaurant; it's a celebration of cultural diversity served on a plate.

Meet the Team:

-Gelila Getu from Ethiopia brings the soulful spices and rich history of East African cuisine.

-Adrianna Bordonaro shares the warmth and heartiness of Ireland through comforting classics.

-Ralph Wakim introduces the exotic flavors of Lebanon, where every bite tells a story.

-Mattia Muggeri brings the vibrancy of Hong Kong to your table, igniting your taste buds.

Together, we invite you to savor the world on a plate at Culinary Concoctions. Join us in celebrating the tapestry of global flavors that weave our story into every dish.
